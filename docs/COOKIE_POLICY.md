## English Version

### 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you use our App. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use the App.

### 2. Types of Data Storage We Use

Since **Stappa Drink Tracker** is a mobile application, we primarily use **local storage** mechanisms rather than traditional web cookies:

#### 2.1 Essential Storage
**Purpose**: Required for the App to function properly
- **Authentication Tokens**: To keep you logged in between sessions
- **User ID**: To identify your account
- **Language Preference**: To remember your chosen language (Italian/English)
- **Session Data**: To maintain your active session

**Legal Basis**: Necessary for contract performance (providing the App service)
**Expiration**: Until you log out or uninstall the App
**Can Be Disabled**: No - these are essential for the App to work

#### 2.2 Functional Storage
**Purpose**: To enhance your experience and remember your preferences
- **Theme Settings**: Dark mode or light mode preference (if applicable)
- **Notification Preferences**: Your choices about which notifications to receive
- **Last Selected Establishment**: To quickly access your favorite venues
- **Form Auto-fill Data**: To remember non-sensitive form inputs

**Legal Basis**: Your consent
**Expiration**: Until you clear app data or uninstall
**Can Be Disabled**: Yes - through App settings

#### 2.3 Analytics Storage
**Purpose**: To understand how users interact with the App and improve performance
- **Usage Statistics**: Which features are most used
- **Performance Metrics**: App load times, crashes, errors
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, screen size

**Legal Basis**: Your consent
**Expiration**: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days
**Can Be Disabled**: Yes - through App settings

### 3. Data We Do NOT Collect via Cookies/Storage

We **DO NOT** use:
- Third-party advertising cookies
- Cross-site tracking cookies
- Social media tracking pixels (unless you explicitly share content)
- Selling or sharing your data with data brokers

### 4. How to Manage Storage Preferences

You can control data storage through:

#### On iOS:
1. Open the App
2. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy**
3. Toggle off "Analytics" or "Remember Login"
4. To completely clear all data: iOS Settings > Stappa > Clear App Data

#### On Android:
1. Open the App
2. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy**
3. Toggle off "Analytics" or "Remember Login"
4. To completely clear all data: Android Settings > Apps > Stappa > Storage > Clear Data

#### Complete Data Deletion:
- Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data
- To delete server-side data, use **Settings > Account > Delete Account**

### 5. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services that store data:

#### 5.1 Expo Push Notifications
- **Purpose**: To send you notifications about new messages, promotions, or account activity
- **Data Collected**: Push notification token
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://expo.dev/privacy](https://expo.dev/privacy)

#### 5.2 Analytics (if implemented)
- **Service**: [Name of analytics service if used]
- **Purpose**: To improve app performance and user experience

- **Opt-Out**: Available in App Settings > Privacy

### 6. Data Security

All data stored locally on your device is:
- Protected by your device's native security features (encryption, biometric locks)
- Not accessible to other apps
- Only transmitted to our servers over secure HTTPS connections
- Encrypted during transmission

### 7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. The App is intended for adults aged 18+.

I cookie sono piccoli file di testo che vengono memorizzati sul tuo dispositivo quando utilizzi la nostra App. Ci aiutano a fornirti un'esperienza migliore ricordando le tue preferenze e comprendendo come utilizzi l'App.

**Base Giuridica**: Necessaria per l'esecuzione del contratto (fornitura del servizio App)
**Scadenza**: Fino a quando effettui il logout o disinstalli l'App
**Può Essere Disabilitata**: No - è essenziale per il funzionamento dell'App

#### 2.2 Archiviazione Funzionale
**Scopo**: Per migliorare la tua esperienza e ricordare le tue preferenze
- **Impostazioni Tema**: Preferenza modalità scura o chiara (se applicabile)
- **Preferenze Notifiche**: Le tue scelte su quali notifiche ricevere

- **Dati di Auto-compilazione Form**: Per ricordare input di form non sensibili

**Base Giuridica**: Il tuo consenso
**Scadenza**: Fino a quando cancelli i dati dell'app o disinstalli
**Può Essere Disabilitata**: Sì - tramite le impostazioni dell'App

**Scopo**: Per comprendere come gli utenti interagiscono con l'App e migliorare le prestazioni

**Base Giuridica**: Il tuo consenso
**Scadenza**: Aggregati e anonimizzati dopo 90 giorni
**Può Essere Disabilitata**: Sì - tramite le impostazioni dell'App
